My Projects

This page is to display some of the micro-projects that I made at university. These projects were designed to teach me game development techniques that are common in popular games.

Couch co-op

For the first project, this was poor in terms of content. However, although the execution was simple, this taught me how to change the camera in Unity to create a split-screen effect.

Drive and Walk

This game taught me two things: vehicle movement, and interacting with objects using the mouse button. I challenged myself to create a spaceship as the "drive" theme, this was used later on in Formidulosus 2.

You Control the Environment (Not the Player)

This was the most challenging assignment. It taught me how to use prefabs, item arrays, and more techneques that I used in later assignments.

I also learned how to make an isometric game, unlike the traditional 2D and 3D games I was making as part of my assignments.
