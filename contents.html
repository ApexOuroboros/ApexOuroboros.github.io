Formidulosus

Formidulosus started as a game design document and later a project for a university assignment. Formidulosus ended up as a small blank box with a capsual enemy with no AI, limited gameplay, and a buggy player, however there was still potential in this project. Formidulosus 2 was my final assignment, I knew I had the ability to improve the origional and this ended up becoming my best project. I used tools I had learned from other assignments and I began to impliment AI to make the enemy "think". I plan to finish Formidulosus in the future as I am passionate about it.
